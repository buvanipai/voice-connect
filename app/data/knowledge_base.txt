Bhuvi IT Solutions is a specialized staffing and consulting firm focused on AI and Nearshore talent.
We do not just do general IT; we focus on AI Engineers, Data Engineers, and Cloud Security experts.
Our primary differentiator is "Nearshore Delivery" using talent from Mexico, Chile, and Argentina.
We can deploy engineers to the US quickly using TN Visas, which are faster and easier than H-1B visas.
Our "NearMind" subsidiary in Mexico handles workspace, legal, and payroll for nearshore talent.
We offer "Travel-Ready" candidates who can fly to the US for onsite work anytime.
We are targeting a bench of 50-60 consultants by March 2026.
For clients, we offer AI Product Development including Chatbots, AI Agents, and Customer Service Automation.
We save clients money by offering senior AI talent at nearshore rates compared to US-based staffing firms.
Bhuvi IT Solutions is a US-based company headquartered at 1699 Woodfield Road, Schaumburg, IL 60173. 
We have a subsidiary called NearMind, which is a LatAm-based company operating in Mexico, Chile, and Argentina. We also have office locations in India and Canada.
